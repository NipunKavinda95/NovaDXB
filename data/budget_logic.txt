MONEY-SAVING TIPS
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Many of Dubai's best experiences are completely free — fountain, beaches, souks, heritage areas.
2. Dubai has zero income tax and low VAT (5%) — prices on menus include everything with no hidden service charges at local restaurants.
3. Eat where Dubai residents eat — JLT food courts, Satwa area, Deira streets — same quality as tourist areas at 40-60% lower prices.
4. Friday brunch packages (11am-4pm) at mid-range hotels often include unlimited food and drinks — better value than paying separately for lunch and dinner.
5. Buy a Nol metro card (AED 25 deposit) on day one — saves AED 1-2 per ride and works on bus tram and some water buses.
6. Summer (June-August) prices for hotels are 40-60% lower than peak season with identical weather indoors.
7. Many attraction tickets are 15-20% cheaper when booked online in advance versus at the gate.
8. The Dubai Pass (available online) bundles 30+ attractions at significant discount for multi-day visitors.
9. Happy hour at Dubai bars is typically 5pm-8pm with 2-for-1 deals — standard across most licensed venues.
10. Duty free at Dubai Airport on departure is genuine world-class value — especially for perfume and electronics.
